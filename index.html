Solar Powered Outdoor Fairy Lights

Create a Magical Ambience with Beautiful Fairy Lights

Illuminate your garden or patio with these stunning fairy lights powered by the sun. No more ugly electric wiring to spoil the look of your outdoor space - these lights are powered by the sun and can run for 6-8 hours, automatically charging during the day.

1. Charge the lights

Find a sunny spot in your garden and insert the solar panel into the ground, making sure it's facing the sun. The lights will automatically charge during the day.

2. Decorate your garden

Arrange the lights around your garden, porch, fence, or any other outdoor space you want to decorate. The flexible copper wire lets you shape the lights however you like.

3. Enjoy a romantic atmosphere

As the sun sets, the lights will automatically turn on, creating a warm and romantic atmosphere in your garden every night. Sit back, relax, and enjoy the magic of your fairy lights!

Solar Powered

  • Charges during the day using solar energy
  • Saves money on electricity bills
  • Environmentally friendly
  • No wires or electricity needed

The solar panel charges the lights during the day using sunlight, so you don't have to worry about running wires or paying for electricity. This not only saves you money, but also helps reduce your carbon footprint.

Multiple Lighting Effects

These solar garden lights come with a range of lighting effects to choose from, including:

  • Twinkle
  • Sequential
  • In Waves
  • Chasing
  • Combination
  • Slow Glow
  • Slow Fade
  • Steady On

With these options, you can create the perfect ambiance for your garden or outdoor space.
